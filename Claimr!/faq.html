Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about MicroSure's services.

What is microinsurance?

Microinsurance is low-cost insurance designed to protect low-income individuals against specific risks such as illness, accidents, or death. It offers peace of mind and financial support when it's needed most.

Who can sign up?

Anyone over the age of 18 with a valid ID or phone number can register. We serve individuals, families, and small businesses.

How do I pay my premiums?

You can pay monthly using mobile money, wallet transfer, or bank card — whichever is most convenient for you.

What is covered in the basic plan?

The basic plan typically covers hospital admissions, minor surgeries, and accidental injuries. For full details, check our Plans page.

How long does it take to process a claim?

We aim to review and pay valid claims within 72 hours of receiving complete documentation.

Where can I get support?

You can reach us via WhatsApp, phone (0800-MICRO-SURE), or email at support@microsure.ng.

Still have questions?

Contact our support team — we’re happy to help.
